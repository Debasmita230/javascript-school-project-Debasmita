ISRO Space Exploration

Formation of ISRO

August 15 1969

ISRO (Indian Space Research Organization) was officially established,with the vision to harness space technology for national development.

First Satellite:Aryabhata

April 19 1975

ISRO launched its first satellite,Aryabhata,marking India's entry into space exploration.

Chandrayaan-1:India's First Moon Mission

October 22,2008

ISRO launched Chandrayaan-1,which made a major discovery:water molecules pn the lunar surface.

Mangalyaan Mission to Mars

Novenber 5,2013

India became the first Asian nation to reach Mars orbit-and the first in the world to do it in the first attempt.

Chandrayaan-3:Historic Moon Landing

August 23,2023

India became the first country to land near the Moon's south pole,showcasing ISRO'S growing technological edge in planetary exploration.
